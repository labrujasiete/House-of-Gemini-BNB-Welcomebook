JACUZZI
Jacuzzi instructions & maintenance
Enjoy responsibly and follow safety guidelines. Keep the area clean, avoid bringing glassware, and limit late-night use. Cover after use and report any issues promptly. Thanks for ensuring a relaxing experience for all.
To Turn On
  1. Locate the control panel near the jacuzzi.
  2. Press the power button to activate the jacuzzi.
  3. Adjust temperature and settings as desired using the control panel.
  4. Wait for the water to reach the desired temperature before entering.
To Turn Off
  1. Press the power button again to deactivate the jacuzzi.
  2. Ensure all jets are turned off.
  3. Replace the jacuzzi cover to conserve energy and maintain cleanliness.
  4. If unsure, refer to the instruction manual or contact the host for assistance.
