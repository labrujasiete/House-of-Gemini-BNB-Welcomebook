BOILER
Boiler instructions & maintenance
Instructions
  1. Find the boiler's controls, usually near the unit.
  2. Flip the ON/OFF switch or turn the knob to activate the boiler.
  3. Ensure the thermostat is set to your desired temperature.
  4. Watch for the boiler's burner igniting to heat water.
  5. Use the temperature control to adjust hot water temperature if needed.
  6. When finished, switch off the boiler.
  7. Be aware of safety features and procedures, including gas leak detection.
